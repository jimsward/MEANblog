A Catch-All MEAN App

I have created a fully functional MEAN app currently in use as a catch-all for a defunct URL to give potential visitors to the defunct site a little info on the situation with the site and its business state..

It consists of a Home page, some links, a contact form that sends me an email and inserts the contact info into a database..

This can easily be extended as the need arises; making use of the database connection, email client, and a basic Single Page App structure.
